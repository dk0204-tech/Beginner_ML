## Statistical Analysis

Performed Chi-Square Test between Chest Pain Type and Heart Disease.

### Hypothesis

- H₀: Chest pain type and heart disease are independent
- H₁: Chest pain type and heart disease are related

The test showed statistically significant relationships between chest pain type and heart disease prediction.

## Key Insights

- Chest pain type strongly influences heart disease prediction
- Maximum heart rate is a major predictor
- Cholesterol and blood pressure impact heart disease risk
- Random Forest achieved strong prediction performance
- Medical attributes significantly affect diagnosis
